What is Anarchy?

The Anarchy Experience

Anarchy servers like VedrunaCraft offer pure Minecraft survival with minimal rules. This means:

  • No protection from griefing or raiding
  • No item or build restoration
  • PvP is always enabled
  • Players can form alliances or go solo
  • The strongest (or sneakiest) survive

Survival Tips

Surviving in an anarchy server requires different strategies:

  • Hide your base well: Use natural terrain to your advantage
  • Spread out resources: Don't keep all your valuables in one place
  • Trust carefully: Alliances can be broken at any time
  • Stay mobile: Have multiple bases in different locations
  • Play smart: Sometimes avoiding conflict is the best strategy
