Server Rules

While we're an anarchy server, we have a few basic rules to ensure fair play for everyone.

Zero Tolerance Rules

  • No hacking/cheating: While we have minimal anti-cheat, blatant hacking will result in removal
  • No DDoS threats/attacks: Immediate permanent ban for any DDoS-related activities
  • No doxxing: Sharing personal information of other players is strictly prohibited
  • No lag machines: Intentional server lag or crash attempts are not allowed

Fair Play Guidelines

  • Use of client-side mods is allowed (Optifine, minimaps, etc.)
  • Autoclickers are permitted but keep it reasonable
  • Alts are allowed but may be limited during high player counts
  • Base griefing and PvP are encouraged - this is anarchy!

Discord Rules

  • No spam in any text channels
  • Keep discussion respectful (minimal toxicity)
  • No NSFW content in non-NSFW channels
  • English is the primary language for public channels

Important Notes

  • Rules are subject to change at admin discretion
  • Admins have final say in all rule interpretations
  • No refunds for donations (if applicable)
  • Your continued play means you accept all rules

Remember: This is an anarchy server. Most things are allowed, but these few rules keep the server playable for everyone.
