FAQ

How do I report a player?

Join our Discord and provide evidence (screenshots/video) in the appropriate channel. Remember, this is an anarchy server - most things are allowed!

Can I get unbanned?

Appeals are handled on a case-by-case basis. Contact us via Discord or email with your username and reason for appeal.

Server is down, what do I do?

Check our Discord for status updates. We aim for 24/7 uptime, but occasional maintenance may be required.
